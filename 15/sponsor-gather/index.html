Sponsor Gather!

Thinking about sponsoring Gather? Well we’d say “do it!” and tell you to email Rochelle to request a sponsorship document, but then we already know and love Gather. If you’re thinking about sponsoring Gather for the first time here are a few reasons why you should consider it.

Gather is a well-loved brand in the professional community.

And we all want to be associated with something we love, right? There’s a tremendous amount of goodwill for the sponsors of the event by being associated with Gather.

More than 1,000 people would like to attend …

… but Gather is limited to only 320 attendees. Getting onboard as a sponsor early means your brand is put in front of all 1,173 (and counting!) in our audience. And of course you’ll get special attention on the day.

Your presence indicates industry leadership.

There are many companies that would like to sponsor Gather but few are chosen. Be the first in your industry vertical to sponsor the event this year.

Find your next awesome employee.

Gather is the perfect place to get in front of potential candidates — no recruiters in sight. Who knows, you just might snag that developer or designer or project manager or ideas person or … (you get the idea) that you’ve been looking for!

Join a select group of companies who identify with similar values.

Gather is about sharing and collaboration and generally being awesome, just like you.

What do I get?

Apart from being hailed as an awesome sponsor on the day, you’ll also get free tickets, your logo on the website & emails, and the opportunity to brand swag items.
