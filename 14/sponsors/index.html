Gold

The Digital Arts Network was ‘founded with a vision that the web was more than just a place to click and read. That it was a place where real conversations could take place’, so it’s no surprise at all that they’re supporting a web-focused conference that’s all about sharing and learning in-person. Choice.

If it weren’t for Sitehost, you wouldn’t be reading this. They are our first choice in hosting and we have never ever been disappointed. We love their customer support and recommend these guys to anyone who asks.

We have a soft spot for Vend because it was launched at Gather. Vend is what clever retailers in the future would use as their point-of-sale system — and you can too, today. No time machine required!

Become a gold sponsor for 2014. Email rochelle@gathergather.co.nz/14 for more information.

Silver

We don’t know where you’ve been in the last ten years if you haven’t encountered any amazing Cactuslab work. Gather is a big fan of Letterboxd, their latest venture. It is the film geek’s BFF after IMDB.

We may be awesome with that Xero reconcile button but we’re nowhere as bad-ass as these guys. Justin and his team at Cloud Accountants will sort out your hairy business finance books quick smart and they have awards to prove it.

InternetNZ is dedicated to protecting and promoting the Internet in New Zealand and fostering a coordinated, cooperative approach to its ongoing development. We’re thrilled to have them supporting Gather in 2014.

We’re a bit biased when we say that Lee ter Wal is one of the best business-to-business design agencies in New Zealand. Su Yin works there. We’ll stop here, the work speaks for itself.

Collecting registrations used to be a monumental challenge. Now, we sit back and watch people register. And because Lil Regie talks to Xero, we see the invoices stack up, get sent out and then magically reconcile when they’re paid. Gather won’t happen without LilRegie.

Microsoft have been supporting Gather since its inception as Barcamp Auckland in 2007. We’re so grateful for their continued support and contribution.

We were pretty excited when Pitch sent us an email asking to be a sponsor because Pitch was created by some young go-getters who decided there was a better way of doing things. Just like us really. :-)

Looking for a free open-source CMS? Look no further than Silverstripe. A home-grown powerhouse for excellence in content management for web, these guys are yet another demonstration of Kiwis taking on the world.

We’re pleased to have Snap Internet on board as a sponsor in 2014. Not only do these guys just get on with the job of supplying fast internet and phone services for homes, business and schools, they also work behind the scenes to quietly support a number of great charities. They’re an ISP with a heart, and we think that’s something worth supporting.

Become a silver sponsor for 2014. Email rochelle@gathergather.co.nz/14 for more information.
