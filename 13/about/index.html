Our Story

Gather was started as Barcamp Auckland in 2007 by a high school student, Ludwig Wendzich. The first event attracted 60 people.

Fast forward five years and Ludwig has since graduated from high school, university and is now working full-time at Lee ter Wal Design. Barcamp metamorphoses into Gather and the team expands to include studiomate Su Yin Khoo.

This year, we launched a new venture in Workshops for Schools. We have over 800 people on the mailing list and the unconference is always sold out.

Gather is growing fast and we now have a mission and a roadmap for the future, as well as an awesome team of people who support it. We are looking forward to the fun adventures in store for Gather and would love for you to come along!
