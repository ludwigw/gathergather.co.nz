What is Gather?

Gather is the creative community’s big day out. Collaborate, learn and share your latest works-in-progress, tech toys, and side projects with interesting, awesome people. You will be fizzing after spending the day among such excellent company.

Gather has all the flavours of your typical unconference, but with a twist, just like every good cocktail.

If you work in the creative space or live on the internet, then you’ll fit in just fine!

Wasn’t Gather called something else?

Yes, the Unconference Previously Known as Barcamp Auckland.

Who are the speakers?

You are! The beauty of unconferences are that everyone gets a chance to share, learn and collaborate their latest works-in-progress, tech toys, and side projects.

The only conference part of Gather is the keynote session by our three special guests.

Who attends Gather?

Past attendees include MPs, musicians, scientists, developers, lawyers, architects, librarians, business consultants, journalists, crafters, game designers, coffee nerds, RPi tinkerers … professionals with many hats and intersecting interests.

If you work in the creative space or live on the internet, you’ll fit in just fine!

Where and when is Gather?

Gather 2013 will be held at Ormiston Senior College, Flat Bush, Auckland on the Saturday, 13 July.

Gather starts from eight till late. It’s going to be pretty full-on but the main complaint we get is that attendees don’t have enough time to talk to all the interesting people they meet on the day!

What can I expect to get?

Your brain exploding with inspiration after sharing and learning about projects and ideas with other super smart people — many whom you may never normally meet.

How will the day be organised?

The schedule is open: add, suggest or edit your session on the Gather schedule.

Sessions are divided into multiple 40-minute blocks to do absolutely anything you want. You’re in the driver’s seat.

What’s a typical session like?

Sessions are not necessarily a one-person presentation. They can be co-hosted discussions, lessons, or even just a sit-down-and-make-stuff. You don’t have to be the expert to host a session. Suggest a topic and see if anyone is interested in helping you run it.

Will there be technology for presenting presentations?

Oh yes! Projectors in every presenting space! They will be XGA (VGA, 1024×768) so bring your appropriate adapters (the Gather team may have limited adapters available).

Do I have to worry about food?

Morning tea and lunch will be provided (if you have a special dietary requirement — please let one of our volunteers know. If you’re still feeling peckish, the Kwik-E-Mart is only fifteen minutes’ walk away.

Mt Atkinson’s coffee cart will be on-site from 8am to 4:45pm.
